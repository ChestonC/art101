How do you pump air into your car tires with low air pressure?

  1. Go to a gas station.
  2. Pay for the machine to pump air.
  3. Connect the extendable wire from the machine to your tire's valve.
  4. Keep wire in place of the valve and squeeze the handle at the end of the wire.
  5. Let go to view the current air pressure.
  6. Repeat steps 4-5 until air pressure is at least 30-35.
  7. Repeat steps 3-6 on remaining tires.

Creating a program that can play Rock, Paper, Scissors.

  1. Create the choice of Rock, Paper and Scissors for the player.
  2. Have the computer be able to choose one of the three options at random.
  3. Program the win condition of Paper beats Rock, Rock beats Scissors, and Scissors beats Paper.
  4. Add win screen for the player.
  5. Add loss screen for the player.
  6. When the player and computer choose the same option, reset the game.

How to change your desktop background (Windows)

  1. Turn on your computer
  2. Log in to your account on your computer
  3. Wait for all the fun startup programs to start up
  4. Right-click on any empty spot in your desktop (empty means not on any icons)
  5. Navigate your mouse to the “Personalize” button and left-click on it
  6. Find the image you are looking for and click on it
  7. If the image you are looking for isn’t there, click on the “Browse” button
  8. Find the image you are looking for using the file browser
  9. NOTE: I’m assuming you know how to navigate the file browser and you know where the image is. If you don’t, then may God help you
  10. Double click on the image you want
